Pros and Cons of AI Writing Tools
Site Author

Abstract
 This article contains information on the main pros and cons that are present when using AI writing tools. The main pros the author brings up for AI writing tools are: data quality, speed, and cost. While the cons for AI writing tools include: lacking an understanding of human complexities and emotions, no creativity or imagination, and lack of accuracy. The stance this author makes on AI writing tools is that they can be beneficial, but at the moment are not perfect.
