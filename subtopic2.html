Ethical Issues in AI
Site Author
 Writing Across the Curriculum


Abstract
 This article details some of the main ethical comcerns of using AI writing tools. The issues the article brings up are: copyright and intellectual property, can writers really take credit for work that they didn't entirely write; availability and equity, specififcally for students AI writing tools raise some concerns for how students can't really develop their writing skills by using AI; privacy and data mining; and bias, as AI writing tools pull information from the internet this means that some of the information gathered lean more towards being opinionated rather than factual.

Abstract
 In this article, it briefly addresses what AI writing tools are, what they're used for, as well as listing of the advantages and disadvantages of these tools. The main topic the article delves into are the ethics of using AI content tools. The issues that the article brings up are, plagiarism, bias, lack of accountability, and possible job loss if AI were to be a big part of a company or service. The article also provides possible solutions or ways to incorporate AI ethically.
