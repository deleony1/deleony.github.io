The Evolution of Writing: AI Tools at the Forefront
Site Author
 James Smith


Abstract
 This article contains information about prominent AI writing tools that are being created. The two main AI services the article talks about is Essayservice.ai and Myessaywriter.ai, which are both services that specialize in things like generating essay or writing topic ideas, or refinining a person's writing. The article takes a stance that AI writing tools can be used as an assistance alongside human written work, rather than to replace human writers entirely.
